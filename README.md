1.  Game was built in _Godot_
2.  All the assets and art is made by hand
3.  Very fun and enjoyable. Has a really good game loop.

**Functionality 👾**

1.  Use W,S,A,D or Arrow keys to move
2.  Try to not touch the humans. If humans touch you they die!
3.  Buy upgrades from the shop! 
4.  Buy different Profile Pictures from the pfp shop!
5.  On the side of the screen: PFP shop, AI Assistant, Leaderboard, etc

**Storyline **✏️****

You are the only robot left on earth. You were an AI (ChatGPT) who lives on with eternal power. All sources of energy are exhausted because of human carelessness. Now all humans want you. They are blinded by the fact that it causes electric shocks when they touch you. Your goal kill the least amount of people in given time. 

**Theme Usage** ⭐

**Beneath the Surface:** This game shows how earth on the surface right now looks good but if it continues like this then there are many problems that can arise. One of them as shown in the game

**Roles Reversed :** The roles have been reversed. Now rather than the user killing the players the players try to kill themselves and you have to get away from them!

**Time:** The whole game is based on how long you can survive.

**Shock****:** The robot shocks the players when they touch him. Also the currency is literally called shocks.

**You can't save them all:** You can't save all the humans. Sometimes you have to sacrifice some humans to save others.
